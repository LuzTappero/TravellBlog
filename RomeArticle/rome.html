    • ROME IN 3 DAYS

    DAY 1 - Arrival and exploring the city
    Start your Roman adventure by visiting the historic Colosseum and the ancient Roman Forum. Marvel at the grandeur of the Pantheon and toss a coin into the Trevi Fountain. Stroll through the beautiful Piazza Navona and visit the impressive St. Peter's Basilica in Vatican City. Don't miss the chance to explore the picturesque Trastevere district.

    DAY 2 - Vatican and art
    Begin your day with a visit to the Vatican Museums and the Sistine Chapel. Explore the magnificent St. Peter's Square and climb to the top of St. Peter's Dome for breathtaking views. Visit the Castel Sant'Angelo and walk along the scenic Tiber River. In the evening, enjoy authentic Italian cuisine in a local trattoria.

    DAY 3 - Ancient Rome and beyond
    Visit the impressive Baths of Caracalla and the ancient Appian Way. Explore the beautiful Villa Borghese and its art gallery. Discover the historic Piazza del Popolo and the nearby Spanish Steps. End your trip with a visit to the iconic Piazza Venezia and the Altare della Patria.
