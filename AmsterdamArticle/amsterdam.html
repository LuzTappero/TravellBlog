    • AMSTERDAM IN 3 DAYS

    DAY 1 - Arrival and exploring the city
    Kick off your Amsterdam exploration by visiting the majestic Royal Palace. Take a relaxing canal cruise to see the city's famous waterways and charming houseboats. Wander through the vibrant Jordaan district and visit the Van Gogh Museum. Enjoy a leisurely stroll in Vondelpark and explore the bustling Dam Square.

    DAY 2 - Museums and history
    Start your day with a visit to the Rijksmuseum and its vast collection of Dutch art. Explore the fascinating history at the Amsterdam Museum and visit the Rembrandt House Museum. Walk through the scenic Begijnhof courtyard and enjoy a coffee at a nearby caf√©. In the afternoon, visit the iconic Anne Frank House and learn about the poignant history of this remarkable young girl. In the evening, explore the lively Leidseplein and its many restaurants and bars.

    DAY 3 - Local experiences
    Visit the colorful Bloemenmarkt, the world's only floating flower market. Take a bike ride through the picturesque canals and explore the vibrant De Pijp neighborhood. Visit the historic Heineken Experience and learn about the brewing process. End your trip with a relaxing visit to the A'DAM Lookout for panoramic views of the city.
