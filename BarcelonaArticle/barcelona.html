    • BARCELONA IN 3 DAYS

    DAY 1 - Arrival and exploring the city
    Begin your Barcelona journey by visiting the awe-inspiring Sagrada Familia and the artistic Park Güell. Stroll down the bustling La Rambla and admire the architectural marvels of Casa Batlló and Casa Milà. Relax at the picturesque Barceloneta Beach and explore the historic Gothic Quarter. End your day by visiting the magical Magic Fountain of Montjuïc.

    DAY 2 - Gaudí and modernism
    Start with a visit to the stunning Palau de la Música Catalana. Explore the beautiful Hospital de Sant Pau, a UNESCO World Heritage site. Visit the famous Casa Vicens and the elegant Passeig de Gracia. In the evening, enjoy the nightlife in the trendy El Born district and have dinner at a local tapas bar.

    DAY 3 - Culture and relaxation
    Visit the bustling Boqueria Market for a taste of local flavors. Spend some time in the serene Parc de la Ciutadella and visit the nearby Arc de Triomf. Discover the cultural richness of the Poble Espanyol and explore the Olympic Park on Montjuïc. End your trip with a relaxing evening at the beach or a visit to a rooftop bar with stunning city views.
