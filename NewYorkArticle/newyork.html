    • NEW YORK IN 3 DAYS

    DAY 1 - Arrival and exploring the city
    Begin your New York experience by visiting the iconic Statue of Liberty and Ellis Island. Explore the bustling Times Square and stroll through the scenic Central Park. Visit the Metropolitan Museum of Art and admire the views from the Top of the Rock Observation Deck. End your day with a walk across the Brooklyn Bridge and enjoy the vibrant atmosphere of the High Line.

    DAY 2 - Culture and neighborhoods
    Start with a visit to the American Museum of Natural History. Explore the charming neighborhoods of Greenwich Village and SoHo. Visit the 9/11 Memorial and Museum and walk through the Financial District. In the evening, enjoy a Broadway show or explore the nightlife in the Meatpacking District.

    DAY 3 - Iconic landmarks and shopping
    Visit the iconic Empire State Building and enjoy the panoramic views. Stroll along Fifth Avenue and explore its luxury shops and boutiques. Discover the cultural richness of the Museum of Modern Art (MoMA). End your trip with a visit to the bustling Chelsea Market and the nearby High Line park.
