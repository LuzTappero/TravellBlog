    • LONDON IN 5 DAYS

    DAY 1 - Arrival and exploring the city
    Start your London adventure by visiting iconic landmarks such as Big Ben, the Houses of Parliament, and Westminster Abbey. marvel at Buckingham Palace and watch de Changing of the Guard ceremony,and stroll through the serene Green Park and Hyde Park. Don't miss the chance to visit the legendary Hard Rock Caf√© and the Queen Elizabeth II Centre. Explore historic Whitehall and catch a glimpse of St. James's Palace.

    DAY 2 - Historical London
    Delve into the rich history of London with a visit to the British Museum, where you can immerse yourself in world history and culture. Explore the bustling streets of Piccadilly Circus, Oxford Street and don't forget to do some shopping at Primark,soak in the vibrant atmosphere of Chinatown, and admire art at the National Gallery.You will also may visit the Royal Opera House. In the evening, treat yourself to a delightful dinner in Covent Garden while enjoying a captivating street performance.

    DAY 3 - Royal London
    Marvel at the magnificent St. Paul's Cathedral, a masterpiece of architecture and history. Explore the historic Tower of London and walk across the iconic Tower Bridge, offering stunning views of the River Thames. Wander along the riverside and soak in the bustling atmosphere of Borough Market, a food lover's paradise. Visit the Tate Modern to admire contemporary art and don't miss the chance to experience the magic of Shakespeare's Globe Theatre. End your day with a ride on the London Eye, where you can enjoy panoramic views of the city skyline.

    DAY 4 - A trip to oxford
    Embark on a journey to Oxford, a city steeped in history and academic excellence. Walk along its enchanting streets lined with beautiful buildings made of golden stone, and explore the prestigious University of Oxford, one of the oldest and most renowned universities in the world. Discover the Bodleian Library, a treasure trove of knowledge, and marvel at the intricate architecture of Christ Church College, which served as a filming location for the Harry Potter movies. Take a relaxing punt ride along the River Cherwell, or wander through the lush greenery of the University Parks. With its blend of academic heritage, stunning architecture, and picturesque landscapes, Oxford offers a truly unforgettable experience for visitors of all ages.

    DAY 5 - Chealsea And Nothing Hill
    Explore the charming neighborhoods of Chelsea and Notting Hill. Start your day in Chelsea, known for its upscale boutiques, art galleries, and beautiful Georgian architecture. Continue your journey to Notting Hill, famous for its vibrant atmosphere and colorful houses. Explore the bustling Portobello Road Market, where you can browse antiques, vintage clothing, and artisanal goods. Visit the iconic Notting Hill Bookshop, featured in the movie "Notting Hill". Afterward, take a relaxing walk through the picturesque streets of Notting Hill, stop for a coffee or a bite to eat at one of the many cozy cafes or eateries along the way.
