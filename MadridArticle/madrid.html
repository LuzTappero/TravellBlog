    • MADRID IN 3 DAYS

    DAY 1 - Arrival and exploring the city
    Start your Madrid adventure by visiting the magnificent Royal Palace and the Almudena Cathedral. Wander through the vibrant Plaza Mayor and the historic Puerta del Sol. Take a leisurely stroll in the beautiful Retiro Park and visit the iconic Crystal Palace. Don't miss the chance to explore the renowned Prado Museum and experience the lively atmosphere of Gran Via.

    DAY 3 - Art and culture
    Begin your day with a visit to the Reina Sofia Museum, home to Picasso's Guernica. Explore the Thyssen-Bornemisza Museum and its impressive art collection. Walk along the Paseo del Prado and relax in the charming El Rastro flea market. In the evening, enjoy traditional tapas at Mercado de San Miguel and watch a flamenco show.

    DAY 3 - History and local life
    Visit the historic Templo de Debod and enjoy the stunning views of the city. Explore the charming neighborhood of La Latina and visit the San Francisco el Grande Basilica. Take a walk through the lively Lavapiés district and enjoy some local cuisine. End your trip with a visit to the Santiago Bernabéu Stadium, home of Real Madrid.
